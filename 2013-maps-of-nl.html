‘13 — Maps of ‘Nieuw Nederland’
What is our definition of a country? Is it a group of people sharing the same values or is it the physical borders that separate us from our neighbours? Nieuw Nederland reveals the country that appears when Dutch characteristics are measured all along the country and beyond it’s borders, whereafter a new country with people sharing the same values shows up.

The maps are a visualization of the idea and do not display true data
